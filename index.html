CodeReach helps young students learn the basics of computer science and programming to prepare them for success in STEM.

Our Goal

In a technology-driven world, we strive to prepare young minds in our community for what the future holds. Programming is a sought-after skill that is, unfortunately, not taught in elementary school. We hope that through this 5-month program, our students will become proficient enough with technology to create anything they want.

Who Teaches

At CodeReach, our instructors are highly qualified high school students. All instructors have finished their computer science and math course with over a 95% and go through an application process to be accepted. We hope that classrooms can become more engaging and less intimidating through a peer-to-peer setting!

How much does CodeReach cost?

CodeReach is completely free. We believe that open access to education is very important, so we will never charge for CodeReach.


What should I bring to class?

We provide school computers, so you do not have to bring your own. With that being said, we highly recommend you bring your own laptop so you can continue to work on what you learned in class at home. If you have any medical issues, allergies, or other accommodations, please let us know! We also suggest you bring a bottle of water so you don't get thirsty.


How do I apply?

Due to a limited number of seats, we offer CodeReach through an application process. To apply, click the "Apply Now!" button at the top of the page.


How will I know if I get accepted?

After applications close on December 2nd, admission decisions will be released on December 4th by email. Classes will begin December 8th.
